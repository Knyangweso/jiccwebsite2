Our Purpose Statement

To Bring People To Christ, Put Them In God’s Family, Develop Them To Christ Like Maturity And Equip Them For Ministry In Church And Mission To The World For The Glory Of God.

Church History

Jesus Impact Centre Church was founded in 2016 in Kenya, Machakos County, Drumvale Community. We are a church deeply committed to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ and we are constantlly motivated by our big vision which is anchored on the Great Commission for the Drumvale Community and beyond. God has called us to minister to His people through His Word, Education and Medical Centres. With God’s help and your active involvement, people’s lives will be impacted positively.
